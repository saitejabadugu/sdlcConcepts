SDLC Life Cycle

There are five stages
1. Project Initiation
2. Gathering Requirements
3. Design
4. Coding
5. Testing
6. Deployment & Maintainance

Two types of development models

1. Agile
a. There will be repeated more than once
b. Development will be done in stages wise
c. Final product takes time although middle executes the process
Ex- Cycle Bike Car



2. Waterfall - 
a. Development can be made once
b. It will take more time
c. It has only one iteration
Ex- Car parts
